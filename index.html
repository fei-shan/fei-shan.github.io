About

I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Applications at Virginia Tech advised by Dr. Kurt Luther. I study computer (AI) supported human information practice and collaboration over historical documents, which is under the human-computer interaction (HCI) research category.

I also work as a Graduate Assistant for the IT division at Virginia Tech Research Center in Arlington, VA. Prior to my Ph.D. study, I received graduate degrees in computer science from Georgia Institute of Technology and the University of Maryland.

Research Interest

I'm interested in exploring (1) common themes of human information behavior with technologies across different fields, and (2) the diversity of it resulting from different perspectives and expertise. I develop systems guided by such knowledge.
